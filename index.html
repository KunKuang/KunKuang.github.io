Biography

Kun Kuang is an Assistant Professor in Zhejiang University. He got his PhD degree from Tsinghua University in 2019. He is keen to promote the convergence of causal inference and machine learning technologies. His research interesting include data-driven causal analysis, high dimensional inference, causally regularized machine learning, stable/interpretable leanring and prediction. He has published more than 10 papers in prestigious conferences and journals in data mining and machine learning.

Research Interests

My research interests include causal inference, machine learning, and data mining. In particular, I am interested in the comninations of causal inference and machine learning, including improving the effectiveness of causal inference with machine learning technologies, and bringing stability and interpretability of machine learning with causal inference technologies.
